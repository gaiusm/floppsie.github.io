1. Python Bot Competition

  • rules
    • you are given three maps in which your python bot has to survive
    • which ever bot kills the monsters quickest wins!
    • give it 4 minutes (max time)
  • the human player may not, shoot or spawn anything
    • can move around though
    • can use ’god’ mode to avoid being killed!
  • open up a terminal and use the command line

  • $ gnome-clocks

  • and select a stop watch
